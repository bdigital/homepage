Cascade Rate Features

Much more than a rate shopping application, our tools enable you to better understand your marketplace. Cascade Rate provides alerts when market conditions present an opportunity for additional revenue.


1. Instant Setup

Automatically discover competitor hotels in your market and start collecting data immediately.

2. Advanced Analysis

We provide useful tools such as map plots, charting, custom views and data export.

3. More Data Sources

We help you analyze multiple channels including your branded site, Expedia, Hotels.com, Travelocity, Orbitz and Booking.com.
